# PlanU — AI-Powered Academic Planner

> Upload your syllabus. Get your entire semester organized in under 60 seconds.
>
> 🌐 **Live Site:** [planu.ca](https://planu.ca)
>
> ---
>
> ## About
>
> PlanU is an AI-powered academic planning tool built for students. This is the early public version of the project — a key piece of the portfolio demonstrating the ability to design and build a complete product from concept to implementation.
>
> ---
>
> ## Features
>
> - 🤖 AI-driven syllabus parsing
> - - ⚡ Full semester schedule generated in under 60 seconds
>   - - 🗓️ Automatic deadline and study session planning
>     - - 🎨 Clean, minimal landing page with receipt-inspired aesthetic
>       - - 📱 Fully responsive
